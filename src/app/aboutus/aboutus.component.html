What we are and our history

   

WELCOME TO OUR AQUA WORLD Aquarius fish club is located in Karthikapally, Haripad, Alappuzha, Kerala , India. Aquarius Fish is a small start-up company founded by Mr Athul B.Raj with the aim of introducing the pisciculture among young generation. Different types of fish seeds like those of MST, Red Tilapia, Chitralada, Anabus, Green Chromide (Karimeen), Basa are available in our farm with reasonable prices. In addition to fish farming, we are also engaged in poultry farming. Option of home delivery for distant areas is currently unavailable. But both Home and Spot delivery is available for the nearby locations. Customers who are interested in visiting and checking the fishes in person are welcome. We can also provide you with the basic instructions and awareness required for various fish cultivation. Even though the company is in the initial stages, we can ensure the best services and ready availability.
